What are the properties of the cell?

Now let's focus on the cell model. The cell is named L23_cADpyr (Layer 2/3 continuous accommodating pyramidal cell).

Selecting this in the Model Description widget displays the properties of the cell. There are 1148 segments (based on the 3D points/radii from the neuronal reconstruction) and 132 segment groups consisting of sections of neurites between branch points as well as larger segment groups such as basal dendrites, axon, etc.

Click on Highlight cell regions in the widget (link is under Click to apply colouring to the cell morphology). The cell should change color indicating the different regions:

 > soma - consisting of a set of flat segments recreating the shape of the pyramidal cell soma
 > dendrites - the basal and apical dendrites
 > axon - the short axon which has been added to the model in place of the reconstructed axon. The cell may need to be rotated to see this.
