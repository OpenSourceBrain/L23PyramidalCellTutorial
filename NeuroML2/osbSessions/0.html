Welcome to the L2/3 Pyramidal cell tutorial!

This tutorial will demonstrate how Open Source Brain allows you to interact with multicompartmental cell models in the following ways:

Visualization
 > Explore the structure of a biophysically detailed cell model in 3D

Analysis
 > View the distribution of active conductances on the cell
 > Plot the activation/inactivation variables of ion channels

Simulation
 > Investigate saved simulation data showing how the cell responds to depolarizing/hyperpolarizing currents
 > Change parameters of the model and run your own simulations!
