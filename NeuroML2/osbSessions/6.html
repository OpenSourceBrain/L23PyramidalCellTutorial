What are the properties of the ion channels?

In contrast to K_Pst, K_Tst is a transient K conductance.

Click on Gate: m, time course (taum) and Gate: h, time course (tauh) for this channel.

From these plots it can be seen that the activation gate opens much quicker (peaking at about 1.2 ms) and inactivates on a shorter time scale (~50ms) as compared to K_Pst.
