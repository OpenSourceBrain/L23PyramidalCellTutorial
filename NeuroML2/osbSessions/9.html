Simulating the electrical activity of the cell

This plot shows traces recoded in the Depolarizing current pulse experiment (the data may take a few seconds to load).

Resizing the window will make the traces easier to see.

There are four traces showing membrane potential at:
 > the soma
 > the end of the axon
 > half way along the main apical dendrite
 > a point on the apical tuft furthest from the soma

Note: a plot like this showing all the traces recorded for the current experiment can quickly be created by selecting Results -> Plot all recorded variables.
