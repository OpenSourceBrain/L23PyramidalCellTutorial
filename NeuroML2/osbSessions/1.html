What am I looking at?

This is a model of a Layer 2/3 pyramidal cell which was originally developed in the Blue Brain Project.

It uses a reconstruction of a L2/3 pyramidal cell neuronal morphology from the Markram lab for its 3D structure, with a set of active membrane conductances from Hay et al. 2011

The parameters of the model were tuned to match electrophysiological recordings from an identified L2/3 (continuous, accommodating) pyramidal cell.

More details on the original model can be found on the BBP Neocortical Microcircuit portal website.
