What am I looking at?

This is a model of a Layer 2/3 pyramidal cell which was originally developed in the Blue Brain Project.

It uses a reconstruction of a L2/3 pyramidal cell neuronal morphology from the Markram lab for its 3D structure, with a set of active membrane conductances from Hay et al. 2011, and the parameters of the model are tuned to match electrophysiological recordings from identified L2/3 (continuous, accommodating) pyramidal cells.

More details on the original model can be found on the BBP Neocortical Microcircuit portal website.

The model was converted to simulator independent NeuroML format (see details here) so that it could be used on Open Source Brain, and that is what is being visualised here.
