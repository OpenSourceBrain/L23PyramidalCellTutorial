What am I looking at here?

This is a model of a Layer 2/3 pyramidal cell which was originally developed in the Blue Brain Project.

It uses a reconstruction of a L2/3 pyramidal cell neuronal morphology from the Markram lab for its 3D structure, with a set of active membrane conductances (due to voltage-gated ion channels) from Hay et al. 2011.

The parameters of the model (channel conductance densities and passive properties like specific capacitance, axial resistance) were tuned to match electrophysiological recordings from an identified L2/3 (continuously firing, accommodating, slightly adapting) pyramidal cell.

More details on the original model can be found on the BBP Neocortical Microcircuit portal website.
