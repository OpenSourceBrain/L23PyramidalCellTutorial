What am I looking at?

This is a model of a Layer 2/3 pyramidal cell which was originally developed in the Blue Brain Project.

It uses a reconstruction of a L2/3 neuronal morphology from the Markram lab for its 3D structure, and the parameters of the model are tuned to match electrophysical recordings also obtained by the lab.

More details on the original model can be found on the BBP Neocortical Microcircuit portal website.
