Simulating the electrical activity of the cell

The Experiments tab at the bottom of the screen lists the completed simulations, or "experiments".

Listed here are 2 completed experiments:

 > Depolarizing current pulse
A 3.5 second simulation during which a 2 second depolarizing current clamp input (180pA) was applied

 > Hyperpolarizing current pulse
Similar to above, but with a hyperpolarizing current clamp input (-100pA)

In both cases a small hyperpolarising current (-70pA) was applied for the full simulation duration to keep the membrane potential at ~-83mV.
