What are the properties of the ion channels?

Now let's focus on an ion channel. K_Pst is a slowly inactivating (effectively persistent) K conductance.

To investigate the properties of the channel, graphs of the voltage dependence of the opening and closing of the gates can be created under Plot activation variables

Click on Gate: m, steady state (infm) and Gate: h, steady state (infh). These show the steady state of the activation (m; increased probability for gate being open with higher v) and inactivation (h; decreases with higher v) variables.

Note: you can hide the legend for the traces which appears on the main graph frame by pressing on the top of the graph window.

Click on Gate: m, time course (taum) and Gate: h, time course (tauh) to show how quickly the gates open/close at different voltages. While the m gate has a maximum time course of approx 50ms, the inactivation is much slower, peaking at about 1.2 seconds, which means the channel will not normally have a chance to inactivate during normal spiking.
