Changing the conductance densities

Try changing the conductance densities of some of the ion channels below.

Fast, transient Na+ conductance on axon (S/cm2)
Persistent Na+ conductance on axon (S/cm2)
Persistent K+ conductance on axon (S/cm2)
Input current (pA)
Delay (ms)
Duration (ms)

Some configurations to try:

 > Remove the high transient Na+ conductance on the axon
Setting this value to 0 should lead to reduced firing frequency. However there is still a persistent Na+ and so a gradual depolarisation leading to spiking...

 > Remove the persistent Na+ conductance on the axon
This removes all spiking in the cell.
