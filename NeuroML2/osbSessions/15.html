Changing the conductance densities and passive properties of the cell

Try changing the conductance densities of some of the ion channels and other cellular properties below. Some scenarios to try:

 > Remove the high transient Na+ conductance on the axon
Setting this value to 0 should lead to reduced firing frequency. However, there is still a persistent Na+ conductance on the axon and so a gradual depolarisation leading to spiking...

 > Remove the persistent Na+ conductance on the axon also
This removes all spiking activity in the cell

 > Change the axial resistance of the cytoplasm
Increasing this (e.g. to 1000 Ohm cm) will confine spiking to axon/soma and suppress spread to rest of cell; decreasing it (e.g. to 10 Ohm cm) will lead to the cell being almost isopotential.
