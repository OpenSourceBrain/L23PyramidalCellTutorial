What are the properties of the model?

OSB can extract all of the physiological properties of the model from the NeuroML files and present them through this interface.

The Model Description contains an outline of the properties of the model, including:

Populations
Which populations of cells are present (in this case just one pyramidal cell)

Cells
The different types of cells in the current network

Ion Channels
The ion channels underlying the active conductances of the model cell

Inputs
The electrical stimulation which will be applied to the cell, in this case current clamp pulses
