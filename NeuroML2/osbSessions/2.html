What are the properties of the model?

OSB can extract all of the physiological properties of the model from the NeuroML files and present them in a more accessible form through this interface.

The Model Description "widget" should have opened on the left, but if not press the button on the top left.

This contains an outline of the properties of the model, including:

Populations
Which populations of cells are present (in this case, a single population with just one pyramidal cell)

Cells
The different types of cells in the current network

Ion Channels
The ion channels underlying the active conductances of the model cell

Inputs
The electrical stimulations which will be applied to the cell, in this case current clamp pulses
