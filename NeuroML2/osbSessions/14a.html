Changing the current input

Try adjusting the amplitude and timing of the current input to the cell and running a simulation.

The status icon for experiments will change from (pre simulation; experiment design phase), to (in the queue on our servers), to (running), and finally to (completed), or if there has been a problem running the simulation.

The simulation should take 30-60 seconds and the membrane potential traces will be loaded and plotted automatically on completion.
